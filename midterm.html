    • KBO teams

  • DooSan Bears-Seoul(1982.1.15)

    • Doosan bears has the same base as LG, so it is quite a rival when the two teams meet. Also, Doosan is very strong team. Long time ago, since KBO just started in 1982, Doosan bears always go to post season.

  • LG Twins-Seoul(1982.1.26)

    • LG twins has the same base as Doosan bears. It has been in a rivalry with Doosan since its foundation. LG and Doosan share same stadium. So when these two team meet, It calls Seoul match. The reason is Doosan and LG share same stadium. So, that's why Doosan and LG become rival.

  • Kiwoom Heroes-Seoul(2008.3.24)

    • In January first, this team change the team name Nexon heroes to Kiwoom Heroes by a new sponcer, kiwoom securities. This team was foundation in Seoul but this team is not in rival with Doosan and LG. Because, this team's home stadium is in Incheon.

  • Lotte Giants-Busan(1975.5.6)

    • Lotte giants has very funny cheering culture. I think Lotte's fans are very passionate. This team has many nickname. But these are not good meaning.

  • Samsung Lions-Daegu(1982.2.3)

    • Samsung lions is one of the team which has eight winning cup. In 1985, 2002, 2005, 2006, and 2011~2014.(8) Especially 2011 to 2014, this time people called samsung dynasty. Samsung Lions was one of the strong team. But some of players make a problem(illegality gambling), so the players involved in this problem were either kicked out or sent to another team. After that, Samsung lions is very weak.

  • KT Wiz-Suwon(2013.1.17)

    • KT wiz is a newly launched team. After launched, this team always got a bad score. This team's home stadium is Suwon Kt wiz park.

  • NC Dinos-Changwon(2011.3.31)

    • This team also new launched team. When this team launched at first year, so powerful. For example, NC Dinos showed powerful game, so this team went to post season in first time. But, in 2018 season NC dinos record the lowest grade.

  • SK Wyverns-Incheon(2000.3.31)

    • This team was final winner last year(2018). This team also one of the team which has many winning cup(4).

  • KIA Tigers-Gwangju(1982.1.30)

    • Maybe KIA Tigers has a lot of winning cup in this league(11). In 2017, this team was final winner. But, last year this team was got disappoinment score.

  • Hanwha Eagles-Daejeon(1986.3.8)

    • This team is my hometeam. In 1999, this team last won in Korea series. After until 2017, this team couldn't go post season. But last year hanwha got an amazing score in league. Also, this team went to post season. Unfortunately, they fail at post season.
